# Contribute

We welcome collaboration from urban tree managers, hardware providers, researchers, software developers, and data-processing teams.

## For cities and urban tree managers

Get in touch if you want to test whether 3D monitoring can support your tree inventories, crown assessment, monitoring, risk screening, growth tracking, intervention evaluation, or future planning.

## For hardware providers

Bring or propose a system for testing under real urban conditions.

## For processing teams

Contribute workflows for tree detection, segmentation, cataloguing, DBH, height, crown metrics, QSM, biomass, uncertainty estimation, or benchmark evaluation.

## For researchers

Help us turn the benchmark into reusable protocols, open workflows, training materials, and publications.
