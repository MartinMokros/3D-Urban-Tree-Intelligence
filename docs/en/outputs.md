# Outputs

3D Urban Tree Intelligence is designed to produce practical, scientific, and policy-relevant outputs.

## Planned outputs

- benchmark tables
- harmonised dataset
- field and processing protocols
- workflow documentation
- figures and videos
- policy brief for municipalities
- dataset paper
- synthesis paper
- training materials

## Main synthesis paper

A high-impact synthesis paper will focus on the fit-for-purpose use of ground-based LiDAR for urban tree monitoring and management.

## Dataset / benchmark paper

A structured paper will describe the benchmark dataset, protocols, sensor comparison, workflow comparison, and operational lessons.

## Policy brief

A practical policy brief will translate the benchmark findings into guidance for municipalities, urban tree managers, and public authorities.
